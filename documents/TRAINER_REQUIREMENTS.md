# TRAINER_REQUIREMENTS.md

## Status

* FROZEN (Version-1)

## Version

* 1.0

## Last Updated

* 20-Jul-2026

---

# 1. Overview

Trainers are responsible for delivering premium coaching experiences and maintaining accurate coaching records. Trainers do not perform operational activities such as subscription management, schedule management or trainer assignments.

---

# 2. Trainer Responsibilities

Trainers are responsible for:

* Conducting coaching sessions.
* Recording session notes.
* Maintaining client progress information.
* Completing trainer check-ins.
* Monitoring client adherence and progress trends.
* Reviewing coaching intelligence reports.
* Reviewing AI recommendations.
* Delivering exceptional coaching experiences.

---

# 3. Trainer Profile Management

Trainers shall be able to:

* View their profile information.
* Update permitted profile information.
* Change their password.
* View their assigned clients.
* View their assigned schedules.

Version-1 shall NOT support:

* Trainer self-registration.

---

# 4. Schedule Management

Trainers shall be able to:

* View their schedules.
* View client schedules.
* View trainer availability information.
* View session reschedule information.

Trainers shall NOT be able to:

* Create schedules.
* Modify schedules.
* Manage trainer availability.
* Perform session reschedules.
* Assign trainers to clients.

> Trainer availability shall be managed exclusively by Super Admins.

---

# 5. Session Management

Trainers shall be able to:

* View upcoming sessions.
* View completed sessions.
* Conduct coaching sessions.
* Record session notes.
* Update session related coaching information.
* View historical session information.

Trainers shall NOT be able to:

* Create sessions.
* Cancel sessions.
* Modify historical session records.

---

# 6. Client Progress Tracking

Trainers shall be able to:

* Record client progress information.
* Complete trainer check-ins.
* View client measurements.
* Monitor client adherence trends.
* Monitor goal progress.
* View historical progress information.

Progress information may include:

* Weight changes.
* Body measurements.
* Goal progress.
* Session adherence.
* Coaching observations.
* Milestone achievements.

---

# 7. Coaching Intelligence

Trainers shall be able to:

* View coaching intelligence reports.
* View AI recommendations.
* View adherence trends.
* View plateau detection reports.
* View projected progress trends.
* View weekly coaching summaries.

Trainers shall NOT be able to:

* Modify AI generated insights.
* Expose internal AI recommendations directly to clients.

> Human coaching decisions shall always take precedence over AI recommendations.

---

# 8. Reports

Trainers shall have access to:

* Weekly progress reports.
* Monthly progress reports.
* Coaching intelligence reports.
* Client adherence reports.
* Session summaries.
* Historical coaching reports.

---

# 9. Notifications

Trainers shall receive email notifications for:

* Account creation.
* Trainer assignments.
* Upcoming sessions.
* Session reschedules.
* Session cancellations.
* Client schedule changes.
* Weekly coaching reports.
* Password reset requests.

---

# 10. Timezone Requirements

1. Trainers shall operate exclusively in the Asia/Kolkata timezone.
2. The application shall automatically perform all timezone conversions.
3. Trainers shall never be required to perform manual timezone calculations.

---

# 11. Access Restrictions

Trainers shall NOT be able to:

* Manage subscriptions.
* Renew subscriptions.
* Manage schedules.
* Manage trainer availability.
* Assign or reassign trainers.
* View sensitive business reports.
* View subscription management reports.
* Perform operational approvals.
* Make AI driven operational decisions.

---

# 12. Version-1 Constraints

Version-1 shall NOT support:

* Trainer managed schedules.
* Trainer managed subscriptions.
* Trainer self-registration.
* Client management by trainers.
* AI based coaching decisions.
* Direct client interaction with AI.

---

# 13. Trainer Principles

1. Trainers own coaching responsibilities.
2. Super Admins own operational responsibilities.
3. Human coaching decisions take precedence over automation.
4. Coaching intelligence exists to assist trainers and not replace them.
5. Exceptional coaching experiences are a core responsibility of every trainer.
