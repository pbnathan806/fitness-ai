# 1. Product Principles

1. Build the Minimum Exceptional Product (MEP).
2. Prioritize premium coaching experiences over feature richness.
3. Prioritize client delight over engineering sophistication.
4. Prefer simplicity over unnecessary complexity.
5. Technology shall quietly enable coaching experiences and shall not become the product itself.

---

# 2. Business Principles

1. Version-1 supports monthly subscriptions only.
2. Fixed recurring schedules are the default coaching model.
3. Clients do not book sessions through the application.
4. Super Admins own all operational decisions.
5. Trainers own coaching responsibilities.
6. Human decisions take precedence over automation.

---

# 3. Technical Principles

1. The client's timezone is the source of truth for all coaching schedules.
2. The application owns all timezone conversions and complexity.
3. All timestamps shall be stored internally in UTC.
4. Historical records are immutable unless explicitly modified by authorized users.
5. Prefer proven and simple technologies whenever possible.
6. Optimize for simplicity before scalability.
7. Build for Version-1 requirements before future hypothetical requirements.

---

# 4. AI Principles

1. AI is an intelligence layer and not an operational layer.
2. AI shall never make operational decisions.
3. AI failures shall never prevent business operations.
4. AI recommendations shall always remain subject to human review.
5. AI shall be used for:

   * Progress analysis
   * Trend analysis
   * Report generation
   * Coaching intelligence
   * Recommendations
6. AI shall not be used for:

   * Subscription management
   * Session scheduling
   * Trainer assignments
   * Operational approvals

---

# 5. Product Experience Principles

1. Clients shall never experience operational complexity.
2. Trainers shall focus exclusively on coaching responsibilities.
3. Super Admins shall manage operational exceptions.
4. Reports shall prioritize coaching outcomes and client delight.
5. Future changes shall affect future records only unless explicitly configured otherwise.

---

# 6. Version-1 Principles

1. When in doubt, choose simplicity.
2. When in doubt, choose human decisions over automation.
3. "NOT NOW" is an acceptable product decision.
4. Every new feature must provide clear business value.
5. If removing a feature does not materially reduce client delight, it probably does not belong in Version-1.
6. Version-1 shall prioritize stability, simplicity and exceptional coaching experiences.

---

# 7. Project Motto

> "We are building the Minimum Exceptional Product—a human-centered premium coaching intelligence platform that quietly enables exceptional coaching experiences through operational simplicity and AI-assisted intelligence."
